about

Fabian is a Ph.D. student at the University of Tennessee, where he is also a research assistant at the Understanding Visual Understanding Laboratory (UVU Lab), under the supervision of Dr. Amir Sadovnik. His research interests include machine learning, DNA sequence processing, computer vision, and plant images. Previously, Fabian was a computer professor at the University of Costa Rica, Atlantic Branch (Turrialba, Costa Rica). In addition to having collaborated as a professor at the Costa Rica Institute of Technology.

Fabian did his master's degree in Computer Science at the Costa Rica Institute of Technology with Professor Francisco Torres-Rojas and his undergraduate studies at the University of Costa Rica.
